About

This is a non-profit based organization, aiming to provide solutions for climate change, agriculture and green tech.

Climate

Climate change is affecting every country in every continent. It is disrupting national economies and affecting lives. Weather patterns are changing, sea levels are rising, and weather events are becoming more extreme. Join us to save the planet.

Farming

What kind of problems do farmers face?

  • Cope with climate change, soil erosion and biodiversity loss.
  • Meet rising demand for more food of higher demand.
  • Adopt and learn new technologies.
  • Stay resilient against global economic factors.
Tech

Green technology encompasses a wide area of scientific research, including energy, atmospheric science, agriculture, material science, and hydrology
